Privacy Policy for StarrySky

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how StarrySky handles information when you use the StarrySky mobile game.

Replace the contact email below before publishing this page to GitHub Pages.

1. App Overview

StarrySky is a casual puzzle game in which players rotate 3D objects, solve visual puzzles, collect hidden stars, and unlock themed levels.

2. Personal Information

StarrySky does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address through normal gameplay.

3. Device Storage and Gameplay Data

The app may store gameplay progress, settings, and unlock status locally on your device so that your game progress can be saved between sessions.

This local data is stored on the device and is not used by us to identify you.

4. Data Sharing

StarrySky does not sell your personal data and does not share personal data with third parties as part of normal gameplay.

5. Ads and Analytics

The current version of StarrySky does not display third-party advertising within the game and does not rely on account-based social features.

If analytics, crash reporting, advertising, cloud save, or other third-party services are added in a future release, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Children

StarrySky is a general audience puzzle game and is not intended to collect personal information from children.

7. Financial, Medical, and Government Services

StarrySky does not provide financial products or services, medical or health advice, or government services.

8. Your Choices

If you want to remove locally stored gameplay data, you can do so by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date shown at the top of this page.
